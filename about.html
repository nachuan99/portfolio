Hello,

I'm Sherry, a multidiciplinary designer. For me, there is more than one way to become a creative designer. I have been learning architecture, drawing and painting. I have been working as an interior designer and website designer. I have also been learning UI/UX design and coding. All of these teach me to observe from different perspectives, to design for users and to combine inspiration and conscientiousness.

My interest in design begins with my fascination with architecture. During the design phase, I learn to consider how people feel and interact with the space. I became more and more interested in this concept of user-centered interaction and design, and I use the design skills I learned in college to develop UI and UX projects, as it combines my love for tech, art, and people.
