Auditorium on Mississipi

A challenging project sited on a bridge to prompt visitors to engage with music and the environment

  1. Home
  2. Architecture
  3. Auditorium on Mississipi

To design this challenging project which is specifically located on a bridge, I begin the design process by studying the reflective property of water. The concept of the auditorium is that it wraps around the pylon so that after visitors enter on the surface of the bridge, they descend into the music hall and get closer and closer to the water surface. Therefore, eventually they can both enjoy the music and fully engage with the dynamic view of the Mississippi River.

Semester: 2016 Fall
Intructor: Frederick Stivers
Skills: Rhino, Revit, VRay, Maxwell, Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, Photography, Physical Model Building, Laser Cut
