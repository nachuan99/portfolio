Hello, I'm Sherry, a multidiciplinary designer

For me, there is more than one way to become a creative designer. I have been learning architecture, drawing and painting. I have been working as an interior designer and website designer. I have also been learning UI/UX design and coding. All of these teach me to observe from different perspectives, to design for users and to combine inspiration and conscientiousness.
