Environmental System

A project to study the environmental context of buildings

  1. Home
  2. Architecture
  3. Environmental System

This project focuses on the environmental condition of an existing site. Using Diva and Vasari to analyze the climate of St. Louis and considering the topography of the context, I design a pavilion to accomadate to the environmental context.

Semester: 2016 Spring
Intructor: Hongxi Yin and Ryan Abendroth
Teammate: Jenny Li
Skills: Rhino, Climate Consultant, Diva, Vasari, Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, Photography, Physical Model Building
