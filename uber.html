Uber

Redesign UI for multiple destinations.

  1. Home
  2. UI/UX
  3. Uber

Design Challenge | Tools: Proto.io, illustrator, photoshop

Uber announced it adds a multiple stops feature in October 2017. From a user's perspective, I find there might be some ways to design the UI in a more intuitive way.

StoryBoard

Jack is having dinner with his friends in a restaurant. The food is good, everything is perfect, but...

When it comes to paying the bills, the calculation seems to be complicated.

Jack types people's names and prices of the dishes into the app.

The calculations are done with simple steps.

Final Design

Insights and Moving Forward

Considering from a user's perspective.

Through interviewing travelers, brainstorming and designing the app, I realize people always have precious memories during their journey, either with their families, friends, or someone they just met. The app acts as a diary, as an album and as a storybook to store those beautiful moments. It also provides a platform for people to share them with others.

Design details.

Travelling is about enjoying the process. For me, designing this app is also a great process for me. I learned how to develop a concept from the beginning, how to think in a user's perspective and how to use design to reach the goal. Both travelling and learning never ends, as:

The journey not the arrival matters."

T.S. Eliot
