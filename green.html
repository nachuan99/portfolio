Greenhouse

A project that considers water, wind, sunlight conditions for different types of plants

  1. Home
  2. Architecture
  3. Greenhouse

Starting with a terrarium that provides all needs for a single plant, the project develops into a greenhouse that considers water, wind, sunlight conditions for different types of plants. The building consists of office area for staff members and circulation for public visitors.

Semester: 2016 Spring
